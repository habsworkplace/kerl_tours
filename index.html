Popular Tourist Places

Kerala otherwise known as god's own country due to it's amazing places of attractions like natural beauty, backwaters, beaches, temples, wildlife, scenic landscapes, climate etc.
"Once in a life, you should pack your bag for a rounnd trip to Kerala" In an email or call, our taxis will be ready to recieve you.

Guruvayur, Thrissur

"Abode of lord Krishna Unni kannan(kannaiyyah), this place which called as bhu loka vaikundam. Name literally means, Guru Pavana Puram, Situated in southern part of Thrissur, Kerala"

Sabarimala

This name came from Veer Sabari, from threthayuga. Here dwells the kaliyuga varada(HariHara Putra) as dharma sastha, a place for all devotees irrespective of religion, caste and creed.

Munnar, Idukki

During summer, the cool climate makes this place an amazing destination. Situated 1600m above sea level, contains large number of British model cottages due to colonial era influence.

Alappuzha Kerala

Serene, moisty and windy climate, in fact fabulous condition for the famous Alappuzha house boat experience. Mouth-watering fresh water fish dishes with Kerala spices is a good combination.

Thekkadi, Idukki

Periyar wild life sanctuary situated here, where there is a variety of flora and fauna visible. There are wild elephants, deer, bison. Trekking, boating and jeep safari is possible for tourists.
